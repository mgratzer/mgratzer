## 🧭 About

I'm Martin, a Staff+ engineer based in Austria. I build software that people rely on. Over 20+ years I had the opportunity to work across web, backend, and mobile, and shipped products used by millions. These days I help teams make architecture and platform decisions that hold up over time, and I genuinely love coding, increasingly AI-assisted.

By day I'm a **Senior Staff Engineer** at a SaaS company serving 10M+ users, leading initiatives from platform foundations to LLM integrations. I also run **[topmind GmbH](https://topmind.eu)** for select consulting engagements. More about the projects I worked on can be found at [https://mgratzer.com/projects/](https://mgratzer.com/projects/).

> I try to keep systems simple, developer experience strong, and AI practical, always in service of higher quality work.

## 🤖 Currently Exploring

I'm fascinated by **agentic engineering** and building systems where AI agents collaborate with humans and other agents to get work done. These days I'm lucky to be exploring how agents can integrate into SaaS products in ways that feel natural and actually useful.

It's early days, and I'm learning a lot about what makes agent-assisted workflows reliable enough for production. There's a lot to figure out!
