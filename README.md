In 20+ years I had the opportunity to work across web, backend, and mobile, and shipped products used by millions. These days I help teams make architecture and platform decisions that hold up over time, and I genuinely love building.

I'm fascinated by **agentic engineering** and building systems where AI agents collaborate with humans and other agents to get work done. Currently I'm lucky to be exploring how agents can integrate into SaaS products in ways that feel natural and actually useful.
